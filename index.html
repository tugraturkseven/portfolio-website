ABOUT

My name is Tuğra Türkseven and I am currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at Bahçeşehir University. I have always been passionate about software development, and this passion led me to develop a project in high school that earned me a full scholarship to university.
During my time as a student, I have been fortunate enough to participate in a number of valuable internships that have allowed me to gain real-world experience in the field. I completed a long-term volunteer internship with Netaş, and I am currently participating in a long-term internship with Orion Innovation Turkey. These experiences have helped me to develop my skills and deepen my understanding of the software development process.
One area that I am particularly interested in is web technologies. I have been working hard to develop my skills in Javascript, HTML, CSS, node.js, express.js, and mongodb, and I am excited to continue exploring this area of development.
In addition to my technical skills, I am also a strong communicator and team player. I believe that effective collaboration and communication are essential to the success of any project, and I always strive to foster positive relationships with my colleagues and clients.
Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me. If you have any questions or would like to learn more, please feel free to reach out.
